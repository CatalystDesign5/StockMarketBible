  • ✖ Emotional Decisions

Strategy Based Traders

  • ✔ Analyse Trades beforehand
  • ✔ Know when to enter, exit, hold

What is Trading strategy?

Trading Strategy is a set of research-driven rules & guidelines to enter or exit any position, in a way such that the risk-reward ratio is optimized.
